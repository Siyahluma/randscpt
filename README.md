# Rands Cape Town Website

## Overview

The Rands Cape Town Website is the official online presence for **Rands Cape Town**, a premium restaurant, shisanyama, bar, and entertainment venue located in Khayelitsha, Cape Town, South Africa.

This project represents **Phase 1** of a larger digital transformation initiative. The goal of this phase is to establish a professional online presence that showcases the Rands brand, venue, food, entertainment, and customer experience.

Future phases will introduce customer accounts, online reservations, event ticketing, staff management, and business operations.

---

## Project Goals

- Create a modern and premium website.
- Showcase the Rands Cape Town brand.
- Promote upcoming events.
- Display the restaurant and bar menu.
- Showcase the venue through images and videos.
- Highlight sponsors and partners.
- Provide business information including operating hours and location.
- Deliver an excellent user experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
