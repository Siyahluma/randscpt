The Rands Story

From a local dream to a Cape Town landmark

Rands Cape Town began with a simple idea: that township culture deserves a world-class stage. We set out to create a space where fire-grilled flavour, premium hospitality and unforgettable music meet — without losing the warmth that makes Khayelitsha home.

Today, Rands is more than a venue. It's a lifestyle destination where families gather for Sunday lunch, friends celebrate birthdays in the VIP lounge, and the city comes to dance on Friday nights.
